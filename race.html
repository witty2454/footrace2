Dice Race

Dice Values: Cost 4- Sided Die = $4, 6- Sided Die = $5, 8- Sided Die = $6, 10-Sided Die = $7, 12-Sided Die = $8.
Dice Value: Return 4-Sided die = $8, 6-Sided die = $7, 8-Sided die = $6, 10-Sided die = $5, 12-Sided die = $4.
Instructions for the game.
To play this game you will need a way of recording how to keep track of your score.
To detertmine who goes first in the game each player must roll a single 20 sided die. Highest roll makes the first move.
Each player will start off with 8 credits. Credits are the currency in the game.
To roll a die you must spend the money for it, after you have rolled you recieve the Return Value of the die.
A turn consist of a roll of 1 die and the results move you on the track towards the finsh at 100.

Dont forget to blow for lucky dice!!!
